DemHC V2 is a platform that aims to automate the role of radiologists/clinical general physicians in diagnosing diseases precisely with high accuracy but without the errors and drawbacks of a human radiologist. It acts as a platform that will assist a human radiologist.

DemHC V2 report solution uses report attributes to predict life based diseases with high accuracy.

DemHC's Chest X-Ray Solution automatically analyzes all adult chest x-rays, identifying signs of abnormalities(pneumothorax and pneumoperitoneum etc) and flags them.

Our crowd sourced blogs/articles aims to make knowledges more accessible and free.

AI technology from traditional technologies in health care is the ability to gather data, process it and give a well-defined output to the end-user. AI does this through machine learning algorithms and deep learning techniques. These algorithms can recognize patterns in behavior and create their own logic
